MyCoolClass (2020–2025)

MyCoolClass was a global teacher-owned platform cooperative, building a democratic alternative to commercial online teaching platforms. At its height, it connected hundreds of ESL teachers and learners across continents.

While the cooperative has now concluded its activities, this page remains as an archive, a tribute, and a reminder of what educators can build—together, beyond markets.
